Elevate Your Decor with the Royal Designs Plug-in Picture Light

This elegant plug-in picture light is the perfect way to illuminate your favorite art, photos, and collections with a touch of sophistication. Designed for both residential and commercial spaces, it adds a dramatic and stylish lighting element to any room.

Designed for Precision

The adjustable slide arm allows you to extend the light horizontally and position it exactly where it's needed, making it ideal for framing artwork or highlighting text. Its directional design ensures focused illumination, creating a refined and artistic ambiance.

Easy Control, Modern Style

With an in-line Hi/Low/Off switch, you can adjust the brightness effortlessly without leaving your current spot. The brushed nickel finish adds a modern, elegant touch that complements contemporary interiors seamlessly.

Efficient and Bright

Equipped with energy-efficient T10 LED bulbs, this light delivers warm and even illumination while reducing energy consumption and the need for frequent bulb replacements. Ideal for long-term use and sustainable living.

Flexible Placement

The 12-foot cord gives you the flexibility to place the light in a variety of settings, whether you're lighting a gallery wall, accenting a bookshelf, or showcasing a cherished photograph. It's the perfect solution for large spaces and specific lighting needs.

Whether you're looking for a versatile gallery lighting option or a sleek adjustable wall light, this product offers the perfect blend of design and function. Add a touch of refined elegance to your space with the Royal Designs Plug-in Picture Light.
