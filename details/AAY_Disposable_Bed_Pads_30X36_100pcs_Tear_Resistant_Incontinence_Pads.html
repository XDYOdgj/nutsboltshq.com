AAY Disposable Bed Pads - Extra Large 30"x36"

Keep your sleeping area, furniture, and floors clean and dry with AAY disposable bed pads. Designed with a 7-layer protection system, these pads offer exceptional leak-proof performance and maximum comfort for your loved ones or pets. Whether you're caring for an elderly family member, a child, or a pet, AAY bed pads are a versatile, reliable, and economical solution that ensures a hygienic and stress-free environment.

Why Choose AAY Disposable Bed Pads?

  • 7-Layer Protection Design for Maximum Leak-Proof Performance
  • Super Absorbent Core that Can Hold Up to 1600ml of Liquid
  • Diamond Pattern Directs Liquid to the Center for Fast Absorption
  • Comfortable and Skin-Friendly with No Latex or Dyes
  • Waterproof and Ideal for Use in Various Settings at Home

Versatile Use for Every Need

AAY disposable bed pads are perfect for overnight use and frequent users. Their extra-large size (30"x36") makes them ideal for incontinence adults, patients, or anyone needing reliable moisture control. The tear-resistant cloth backing and durable construction ensure long-lasting performance, while the waterproof properties keep your space clean and safe. Available in 100-piece packs, these pads are a practical and cost-effective choice for everyday use.

Eco-Friendly and Easy to Use

Lightweight and easy to dispose of, AAY bed pads are perfect for home, healthcare, and pet environments. Their design allows for quick absorption and locking of liquids, preventing any moisture buildup on surrounding surfaces. The skin-friendly materials ensure a comfortable experience for users of all ages, while the seamless construction reduces the risk of irritation or discomfort.

Ideal for Incontinence Adults and Patients
