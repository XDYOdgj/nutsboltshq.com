Mudder 3 Pcs Universal Sink Drain Strainer Set

Keep your kitchen sink clean and free of clogs with this reliable 3-piece strainer set. Designed for everyday use, it offers a perfect solution for removing food particles, small debris, and other kitchen scraps efficiently and safely. The universal fit ensures it works with most standard 3.5 inch sink drains in the US, making installation and maintenance a breeze.

Key Features

  • Universal Fit for Most 3.5 Inch Kitchen Sink Drains
  • Anti-Clogging Design with 2mm Holes for Efficient Drainage

This set is perfect for anyone looking to maintain a clean and smoothly functioning kitchen sink.
