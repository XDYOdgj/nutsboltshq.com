Premium LED Strip Diffuser - Black U Channel for Even Light Output

Elevate your lighting design with this high-performance LED strip diffuser. Engineered for both residential and commercial applications, it combines durability with advanced functionality to deliver a clean, consistent glow. The 6063 anodized aluminum extrusion ensures long-lasting strength and efficient heat dissipation, helping your LED lights perform better and last longer.

Designed for Versatile Installation

Whether you're using it for under-cabinet lighting, wall-mounted fixtures, or counter illumination, this diffuser offers a seamless fit. Its snap-fit construction makes setup quick and secure, while the included end caps with and without holes allow for flexible configurations. The bracket doubles as an extension connector or mitre, enabling easy corner installation without compromising the integrity of the lighting system.

Smart Protection for Your LED Strips

The milky PC cover provides an even light distribution while protecting your strips from dust, moisture, and physical damage. With a 90% light transmittance rate, you get a natural, soft illumination that enhances the ambiance of any space. Built with a removable protective film, it ensures your diffuser remains scratch-free during storage and transportation.

Customization Made Easy

Want a perfect fit for your unique setup? This diffuser allows for easy trimming with tools like a hacksaw or grinder. The milky cover can also be cut using utility knives or scissors, giving you full control over the light output. Perfect for steady color, RGB, RGBW, and programmable LED strips (excluding hue 2nd), it adapts to a wide range of lighting needs.

Built to Last in Any Environment

Constructed with stainless steel screws and clips, this diffuser is ideal for use in humid or challenging environments. Its robust build ensures reliable performance, making it a smart investment for anyone looking to achieve professional-grade lighting results at home or in business spaces.
