MOOITZ Folding Security Gate

Keep your home safe with a versatile, durable, and easy-to-use folding security gate. Designed for both indoor and outdoor applications, this gate offers a secure solution wherever you need it most.

Why Choose MOOITZ?

Whether you're looking to secure your garage, create a safe play area in your backyard, or divide a large room into smaller sections, the MOOITZ Folding Security Gate delivers reliable performance with a smart, flexible design. Its expandable frame allows you to adjust the gate from 12.7" to 130", making it adaptable to various spaces and needs.

Durable and Reliable Construction

Engineered with high-quality iron edge tubes and aluminum alloy grids, this gate is built to withstand the test of time and everyday wear. The sturdy materials ensure a long-lasting, strong barrier that keeps your family and pets safe without compromising on style or strength.

Convenient and Safe Design

The MOOITZ gate features a handle and an automatic latch, making it simple to open, close, and secure. Its casters allow effortless movement, and the adjustable feet provide stability on uneven surfaces. The thickened brackets ensure the gate remains firm and secure during use, offering peace of mind in any setting.

Easy to Install and Store

With a straightforward installation process, this gate is ideal for both DIYers and professionals. When not in use, it folds compactly, making it easy to store and transport. This makes it a perfect choice for those who need a mobile, safety-focused barrier that can be moved or adjusted as needed.

Versatile Applications

From securing entryways to partitioning large areas, the MOOITZ folding security gate is your go-to solution for creating a safe, adaptable environment. It's especially useful in garages, courtyards, and other areas where mobility and security are essential.

Key Features:

  • Expandable from 12.7" to 130" for versatile use
  • Built with durable iron edge tubes and aluminum alloy grids
  • Equipped with 360-degree rotating casters for easy mobility
  • Includes wall mounting hardware for added security
  • Folds compactly for convenient storage and portability
