VANoopee 10FT H Track Lighting Rails Kit

This comprehensive track lighting solution is perfect for creating a sleek and functional lighting display in your home or commercial space. With its durable construction and easy installation, it's ideal for both professionals and DIYers who want to personalize their lighting setups.

What's Included

The kit features three 3.3FT rails that extend to a total of 10FT, along with three mini 'I' connectors, three live end connectors, three end caps, and six screws. A detailed instruction manual ensures a smooth and hassle-free setup process.

Why Choose This Kit

Crafted from premium materials, these rails are built for long-term use and can withstand the rigors of daily operation. They are compatible with single circuit 120V H-style track lighting systems, allowing seamless integration with your existing fixtures. The modern black finish adds a stylish touch, while the live end feed connector ensures efficient power distribution.

Customization at Its Best

With a flexible track system, you can easily rearrange or expand your lighting setup to match your space. Whether you're looking to upgrade your current system or start fresh, this kit provides the perfect foundation for dynamic and personalized lighting solutions.

Warranty & Support

Backed by a 3-year warranty, this kit ensures that your lighting investment is protected and reliable. You can enjoy peace of mind knowing that your purchase is supported for years to come, offering lasting performance and satisfaction.
