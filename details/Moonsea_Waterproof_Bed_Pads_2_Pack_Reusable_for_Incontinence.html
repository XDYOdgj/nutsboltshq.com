Moonsea Waterproof Bed Pads

Get the perfect blend of comfort and protection with Moonsea’s premium bed pads. Designed for incontinence, potty training, or pet care, they offer reliable support that keeps your mattress, bed, or floor clean and dry at all times.

Each pad features a four-layer construction that includes a soft brushed polyester top for ultimate comfort, a moisture-absorbing middle layer to keep things dry, a durable waterproof barrier to prevent leaks, and a non-slip base to ensure they stay securely in place. The 34 x 36 inch size provides full coverage, making them ideal for adults, children, women, or even puppies.

These reusable incontinence pads are built to last over 300 washes without losing their function or softness. They are not only waterproof and absorbent but also easy to clean with a simple machine wash and dry routine. Whether you're staying at home or away, Moonsea bed pads give you peace of mind with their leak-proof design and everyday reliability.
