Hapfish Rechargeable Magnetic LED Wall Strip Light

Transform your space with the Hapfish Rechargeable Magnetic LED Wall Strip Light. Designed for both style and practicality, these wall lights offer adjustable brightness and color temperature to create the perfect lighting atmosphere in your home. Whether you're lighting up a cozy living area, a modern kitchen, or a creative workspace, these LED strips provide a versatile and energy-efficient solution.

With 70 high-brightness beads, this light strip delivers up to 300 lumens, making it great for tasks like reading, makeup application, or showcasing your artwork. The dimmable feature lets you tailor the lighting to your mood or activity, ensuring comfort and usability in any setting. Enjoy intuitive control with touch buttons and a remote, and the auto memory function lets you quickly return to your preferred brightness and color settings.

This wireless LED strip bar features a strong built-in magnet for easy installation on metal surfaces. The included adhesive snaps, screws, and user manual make setup simple and hassle-free. The water-based adhesive ensures a secure hold without harming your walls or furniture. Plus, with a 4000mAh rechargeable battery, you get up to 60 hours of operation on low power. Choose from multiple color temperatures and brightness levels to find the perfect blend for your needs.
