HE COSMOSTAR CosmoMix Square Static Mixer Nozzle Tip

Transform your adhesive application process with the CosmoMix Square Static Mixer Nozzle Tip. Designed for use with two-part adhesives such as epoxy and polyurethane, this nozzle delivers seamless, air-free mixing to ensure stronger, more durable bonds every time.
