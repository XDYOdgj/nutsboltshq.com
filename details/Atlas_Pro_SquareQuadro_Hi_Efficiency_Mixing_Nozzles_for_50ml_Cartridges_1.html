Atlas Pro Square/Quadro Hi-Efficiency Mixing Nozzles

Precision meets efficiency with our advanced mixing nozzles designed for all standard A-System 50ml dual-barrel cartridges. Ideal for both DIY projects and professional use, they ensure perfect adhesive mixing every time.

Key Features

  • Hi-efficiency mixing to reduce adhesive waste by 50%
  • Single-use design for cost-effective application
