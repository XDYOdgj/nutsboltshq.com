Shurtape PC-622 Contractor Grade Duct Tape

This reliable, heavy-duty duct tape is trusted by professionals and DIY enthusiasts for its exceptional performance. With a 2-inch width and 60-yard length, it's designed for a wide range of applications, from sealing corners to securing seams with precision.

Featuring a strong natural rubber adhesive, this duct tape provides a firm grip on surfaces such as concrete, metal, and wood. It's the perfect choice for tasks that demand durability, including construction, repairs, and masking projects that expose tough materials like stainless steel.

The polyester/cotton blend cloth carrier ensures excellent tensile strength and flexibility, making the tape resistant to tearing and easy to handle. Its smooth polyethylene backing also allows for effortless cleaning and maintenance during use.

Built for strength, built for performance.

Whether you're tackling a large construction job or a small home improvement, the Shurtape PC-622 is the go-to solution. The 56 ounces per inch adhesion strength guarantees that this masking tape holds up against extreme weather and heavy-duty use, offering lasting reliability for any project.

With its professional white color and compact 3-inch core, this 60-yard duct tape roll maintains a clean, organized appearance on your workbench. It's an essential tool for any contractor's toolbox, ensuring efficiency and effectiveness in every task.
