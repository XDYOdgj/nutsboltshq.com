Vintage Wall Light for Timeless Elegance

The Acclaim Lighting 8423AB is a masterfully designed wall light that brings sophistication and charm to any setting, whether inside or outside your home. Crafted from solid brass and finished with an aged brass patina, it's a classic statement piece that perfectly complements rustic and traditional interior and exterior designs.

Key Features

  • Vintage solid brass construction for durable, timeless appeal
  • Two light, dimmable design with clear glass shades for soft illumination
  • UL outdoor wet rated certification for safe use in rain or damp environments
  • Hand crafted colonial America style with aged brass finish
  • Easy installation with standard back plate dimensions

Why Choose This Light?

With its warm and diffused glow, this light is ideal for patios, porches, barns, bars, and other outdoor spaces, creating a cozy, inviting ambiance. The dimmable feature allows you to customize the lighting to match your mood or occasion, making it incredibly versatile for both residential and commercial environments.
